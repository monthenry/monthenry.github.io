About Me

I am a final-year engineering student at INSA Lyon, majoring in Computer Science, with a concurrent Bachelor in Economics from Université Paris Dauphine-PSL. My academic journey reflects my deep interest in both technology and finance. Since the rise of Bitcoin in 2017, I have been particularly drawn to cryptocurrencies and therefore blockchain technology, this fueled my interest in CeFi and DeFi, ultimately leading me to pursue economics alongside my engineering studies. Throughout my education, I have taken on several data engineering intern roles, working on decentralized systems and blockchain technologies. These experiences have sharpened my skills in problem-solving and critical thinking, as I enjoy tackling technical challenges and seeing projects through from inception to completion. I thrive in collaborative environments, where I can contribute ideas and take the initiative, while also learning from the perspectives of others. I look forward to applying my diverse skills to innovative projects that bridge technology and finance, particularly within the decentralized space.

Portfolio
Incentive Mechanisms in Decentralized Marketplaces

During my research internship at LIRIS (in collaboration with iExec), I explored incentive mechanisms for decentralized computing marketplaces, ensuring quality resource sharing using game theory models. I worked on:

  • State-of-the-art analysis of incentive methods.
  • Modeling attack scenarios and designing governance mechanisms.
  • Developing a "blind slashing" mechanism and conducting simulations for reputation management.
Blockchain-based Student Attendance Management (STAMP)

In collaboration with a teacher during my Erasmus program at Tartu University, I co-authored a paper proposing a blockchain and DAO-based STudent Attendance Management Platform (STAMP). The system decentralizes attendance validation and incentivizes accurate reporting.

Blockchain Data Indexing for Ki Chain

As a Data Engineer Intern at Ki Foundation, I worked on a blockchain indexing project. My key contributions included:

  • Forking an indexer from the Cosmos blockchain ecosystem and adapting it to Ki Chain.
  • Extracting and indexing blockchain transaction data via an API for efficient data distribution.
Construction Data Management Dashboard

At Razel-Bec (Fayat), I worked on a project to streamline the management of construction equipment data. My role involved:

  • Building a REST API to collect, process, and distribute equipment data such as fuel consumption and usage statistics.
  • Developing a dashboard for real-time data visualization to support operational decision-making.
OnTable: Contactless Menu Platform

I co-founded and developed OnTable, a digital platform providing contactless menus for restaurants. My contributions included:

  • Contributing to the development of the full-stack platform using Python Django and AWS for cloud infrastructure.
  • Providing a seamless digital experience for customers and empowering restaurants to reduce physical contact during the pandemic.
